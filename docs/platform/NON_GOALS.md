# MVP non-goals

- proprietary vehicle market-data feeds;
- price-to-market recommendations;
- automated appraisal or acquisition bidding;
- full reconditioning/shop management;
- parts inventory;
- payroll or general ledger accounting;
- recurring loan/lease/RTB repayment servicing;
- automated collections or repossession workflows;
- short-term rental operations;
- public tenant signup or subscription billing;
- native iOS/Android application;
- offline editing;
- drag-and-drop document, workflow, or formula builders;
- lender-network submission;
- digital signatures;
- fully automated marketplace posting or negotiation.
