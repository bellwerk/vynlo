# Personas and jobs to be done

## Dealer owner / workspace administrator

Configure locations, users, roles, inventory rules, integrations, templates, tax packs, and reporting; monitor failures; approve sensitive changes; understand inventory and sales performance.

## Salesperson

Use inventory, leads, customers, appointments, deals, documents, deposits, and listing status from a phone without access to protected administration.

## Inventory coordinator

Create inventory units, decode VINs, manage costs/readiness, upload/order photos, transfer vehicles, and resolve sync issues.

## Sales manager

Review pipeline, pricing, deals, aged inventory, lead assignments, approvals, and audit history.

## Finance coordinator

Record external lender applications, returned terms, conditions, approval expiry, funding, and required documents. Vynlo does not underwrite, calculate, or service the lender's loan.

## Accountant / read-only reviewer

Run stable exports and trace rows to deals, documents, tax snapshots, and transactions.

## Vynlo platform operator

Provision packs, handle incidents, and perform strictly controlled support without silent data access.
