# CRM, leads, tasks, and appointments

A lead records source, interested inventory, party/contact details, assignee, workflow state, next action, consent/contact notes, and conversion data.

Timeline activities include note, call, email reference, text reference, appointment, assignment, status change, document, and deal event. External bodies are referenced rather than copied unless necessary.

Tasks include assignee, due time, priority, state, related entities, and reminder. Appointments include time, location/remote details, attendees, outcome, no-show/cancel state.

Lead conversion links the existing party and timeline to a deal and is idempotent. Contact preferences, do-not-contact flags, consent source, and sensitive notes are permission-controlled and auditable.
