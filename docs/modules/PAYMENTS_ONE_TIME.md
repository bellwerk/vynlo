# One-time deal transactions

Included types: deposit, receipt, balance received, refund/reversal, trade-in credit representation, third-party lender proceeds, and tenant-configured one-time transaction.

Record amount/currency, type, method, status, provider/reference, occurred/settled times, actor/approver, proof file, notes, and related deal/document.

A settled transaction is not edited; reversal/refund creates a linked event. Refunds require permission, reason, and step-up where configured. Aggregate status is derived from transactions, not a free checkbox. Vynlo core does not allocate principal/interest or maintain recurring schedules.
