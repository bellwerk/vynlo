# Exports and reporting

Core reports:

- inventory count/value by location/state;
- aging and days in stock;
- acquisition/added costs;
- asking/sale price and estimated/realized gross;
- leads by source/status/assignee;
- appointments and conversion;
- deals by type/status/location;
- external finance outcomes;
- listing/sync health.

Versioned export definitions specify source, columns/labels, mappings, safe calculated columns, filters/sort, sensitivity/permission, CSV/XLSX, and formatting.

Runs record definition version, filters, actor, row count, checksum, expiry, and audit. Vynlo does not hardcode a dealership's bookkeeping layout; tenant accounting exports use the generic engine.
