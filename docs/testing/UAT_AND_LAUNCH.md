# UAT and launch

UAT covers onboarding/MFA, permissions, mobile/desktop inventory, VIN failure/manual continuation, concurrent stock allocation, image pipeline, listing publish/update/unpublish and retry/conflict, leads/appointments/conversion, cash and external-finance deals, one-time transactions/reversals, document preview/official/signed/void/supersede, reports/exports, isolation, session expiry, and step-up.

Launch requires no critical/high security findings, successful restore, validated staging mappings, approved enabled tax/document/formula versions, accepted migration dry run, assigned incident owners, and tested rollback/feature-disable actions.
