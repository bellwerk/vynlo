# Source and approval notes

## Official source references

The candidate rate and trade-in behavior were derived from Revenu Québec guidance available on the decision date:

1. **Basic Rules for Applying the GST/HST and QST**  
   Revenu Québec identifies the GST rate as 5% and the QST rate as 9.975%, and explains that QST is calculated on the selling price excluding GST.  
   Source: `https://www.revenuquebec.ca/en/businesses/consumption-taxes/gsthst-and-qst/basic-rules-for-applying-the-gsthst-and-qst/`

2. **Trade-ins of Used Road Vehicles — Purchaser Not Required to Collect the GST or Calculate or Collect the QST**  
   Revenu Québec describes conditions under which a dealer calculates GST and QST on the difference between the vehicle selling price and the trade-in credit.  
   Source: `https://www.revenuquebec.ca/en/businesses/consumption-taxes/gsthst-and-qst/special-cases-gsthst-and-qst/transportation-applying-the-gst-and-qst/road-vehicles-businesses/trade-ins-of-used-road-vehicles/purchaser-not-required-to-collect-the-gst-or-calculate-or-collect-the-qst/`
